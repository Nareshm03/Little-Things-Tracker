Keep reading.

But there isn't a single moment where someone instinctively stops.

If this were my project, I'd add one interaction somewhere around Chapters 5–7:

A folded letter that takes 3–4 seconds to fully unfold, revealing a handwritten note.
A cassette tape that plays a 10-second real voice message when clicked.
A tiny envelope hidden behind a Polaroid containing a single line that only appears if the reader lifts the photo.

One deeply emotional interaction is more memorable than twenty clever animations.
