🎬 Chapter 7
The Little Things We Never Want To Forget

Not because they were the biggest moments.

Because they quietly became part of who you both are.

Emotion

If someone cries in this scrapbook...

It should be here.

Not because something sad happens.

Because they suddenly realize:

"This wasn't about dates.

It was about building a life."

Visual Identity

Everything changes.

Every previous chapter was full of objects.

This one is almost empty.

Lots of breathing room.

Cream paper.

Soft morning light.

No desk.

No kitchen.

No temple.

No planning board.

Just...

memories returning one last time.
