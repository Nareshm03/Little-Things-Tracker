The real problems
❌ 1. Font sizes are too small

I'm seeing fonts around 8px–11px in many places.

On a laptop that's almost impossible to comfortably read.

Use something like:

Titles: 52–64px
Chapter subtitles: 22–28px
Main handwritten notes: 18–22px
Body text: 16–18px
Captions: 14–16px
Tiny annotations: 11–12px minimum

Nothing important should ever be below 14px.

Rules:

• Increase all body text by 20–35%.

• Increase handwritten notes by 25%.

• Increase photo sizes by 25–40%.

• Hero photos should dominate the page.

• Reduce unnecessary empty space.

• Fill 75–85% of every spread with meaningful content.

• Keep breathing room, but remove wasted space.

• Scrapbooks are rich and layered—not sparse galleries.

• Every chapter should comfortably fill the two-page spread.

• No important text smaller than 14px.

• Quotes should be immediately readable without zooming.

• Captions should be readable from normal desktop viewing distance.

• Rebalance layouts after scaling so nothing overlaps incorrectly.
