Motion Review

Animations are consistently calm, which fits the theme.

Areas to polish:

Use fewer linear movements.
Add more easing into paper-related motions.
Let objects settle naturally instead of stopping abruptly.
Make small decorative elements (dust, flowers, light) move independently.
UI Consistency

Very consistent.

The typography, spacing, and color palette hold together across chapters.

One thing to watch:

Some chapters are more content-heavy than others. Aim for a more consistent reading rhythm.
