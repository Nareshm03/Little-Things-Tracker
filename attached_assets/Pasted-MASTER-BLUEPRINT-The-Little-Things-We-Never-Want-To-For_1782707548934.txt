MASTER BLUEPRINT

The Little Things We Never Want To Forget

Version

v1.0 – Premium Cinematic Digital Scrapbook

Goal

When Meghana opens this website, she should feel like she’s holding a real handmade scrapbook.

Not a webpage.

Not an app.

A memory.

⸻

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Three words define the entire project:

Warm. Personal. Timeless.

Every design decision must answer one question:

“Would this exist inside a handmade scrapbook?”

If not,

don’t add it.

MOBILE EXPERIENCE

Design mobile-first.

The scrapbook should still feel like a book on a phone, not a squeezed desktop layout.

Increase touch targets.

Avoid hover-only interactions.

Test on common phone widths.

⸻

VERSION CONTROL

Every milestone gets its own commit.

Every chapter should be independently testable.

Never mix refactoring with new features in the same commit.

⸻

FINAL EMOTIONAL GOAL

When Meghana reaches the last page, she shouldn’t feel like she “finished a website.”

She should feel like she closed a book filled with moments the two of you actually lived.

If you achieve that feeling, the project has succeeded.
