My recommendation

If your goal is to make this Awwwards-level or something that genuinely impresses senior designers and engineers, I wouldn't spend the next week tweaking font sizes or shadows.

I'd focus on three areas:

Cinematic page transitions so it feels like turning the pages of a physical scrapbook rather than scrolling a webpage.
Narrative pacing by giving each chapter its own emotional identity and rhythm instead of using similar reveal patterns throughout.
Micro-details such as realistic paper behavior, subtle texture, and tactile interactions that make the experience feel handcrafted.
